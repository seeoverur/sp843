Coffee on King Street

Sweet Palm Coffee is a locally loved café located on King Street in Charleston, South Carolina. Our shop blends modern café culture with Charleston hospitality, serving handcrafted espresso drinks, ceremonial matcha, fresh pastries, and light café meals in a relaxed downtown setting. Whether you’re starting your morning before exploring the historic district, meeting friends for coffee, or stopping in during a King Street shopping day, Sweet Palm offers a welcoming place to slow down and enjoy great coffee.

Espresso & Coffee

From smooth lattes and cappuccinos to bold espresso shots, our coffee menu focuses on quality ingredients and consistent craft.

Matcha & Specialty Drinks

Sweet Palm is known for ceremonial matcha and specialty café drinks that bring a fresh perspective to the Charleston coffee scene.

Café Bites

Enjoy pastries, wraps, and light café dishes designed to pair perfectly with coffee for breakfast, lunch, or an afternoon break.

A Downtown Charleston Coffee Stop

Located on iconic King Street, Sweet Palm Coffee sits in the center of Charleston’s shopping and dining district. Visitors exploring downtown Charleston often stop in for a quick espresso or a quiet moment before continuing through the historic city streets.

With its walkable location, welcoming atmosphere, and thoughtfully crafted drinks, Sweet Palm has quickly become a favorite stop for locals, students, and travelers alike.

Coffee Catering & Mobile Espresso Bar

Sweet Palm also offers mobile espresso catering throughout Charleston and the Lowcountry. From weddings and corporate events to private celebrations, our mobile espresso bar brings the café experience directly to your event.
