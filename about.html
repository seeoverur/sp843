About This Coffee Blog

Welcome to SP843, your new go-to resource for discovering the vibrant and rapidly growing coffee scene in Charleston, SC. This site functions as a community-focused coffee blog, dedicated to reviewing, highlighting, and sharing stories about the best independent cafes in the downtown area, with a special emphasis on **King Street**.

We aim to offer an honest, local perspective on everything from the quality of the espresso to the best places for remote work. Whether you are a resident looking for a new spot or a visitor seeking the perfect cup, SP843 is here to guide your caffeine journey through the 843 area code.
