A Charleston Coffee Shop with a Simple Goal

Sweet Palm Coffee exists for the everyday moments: the first sip before work, the mid-morning catch-up, the afternoon reset, and the easy stop-in while walking King Street. We focus on consistent craft, quality ingredients, and friendly service — the kind that makes you want to come back.

Our menu is centered around espresso drinks, matcha, and rotating café favorites, with simple food options to match. Some days you’re here for a quick coffee to go — other days, you’re here to slow down and stay awhile.

What You’ll Find

  • Handcrafted Espresso
    Lattes, cappuccinos, americanos, cold brew, and more.
  • Matcha & Specialty Drinks
    Ceremonial-style matcha and seasonal café favorites.
  • Café Bites
    Pastries, wraps, and grab-and-go options (selection may vary).
  • A King Street Location
    Easy to visit while shopping, sightseeing, or exploring downtown.

In the Heart of Downtown Charleston

King Street is one of Charleston’s most walkable corridors — known for shopping, dining, and classic historic-district energy. Sweet Palm Coffee is an easy stop for visitors and a reliable go-to for locals who want a great drink without the fuss.
